About Sign Language Translator

Our Mission

At Sign Language Translator (SLT), our mission is to break down communication barriers and foster inclusivity for individuals with verbal and hearing disabilities. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to communicate effectively and connect with others, regardless of their abilities.

Through cutting-edge AI technology and a user-friendly platform, we're working to make sign language translation accessible to all, promoting understanding and empowerment in communities worldwide.

Our Technology

SLT utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms and computer vision technology to provide accurate, real-time translations of sign language to text and speech. Our system is constantly learning and improving, thanks to our dedicated team of researchers and the valuable input from our growing community of users.

We support multiple sign languages from around the world and are continually expanding our language database to serve a global audience.
